FAQ

Common Questions Before Contacting Us

How quickly will MPK Digital respond to my enquiry?▾
We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on business days (Monday through Saturday). WhatsApp is our fastest channel — we typically reply within 2 hours during business hours. Email responses arrive within one business day. We read and respond to every message personally — no bots, no automated replies.
Do I need to know exactly what I want before contacting you?▾
Not at all. Many of our best client relationships started with a vague brief. Tell us your goal — what problem you're trying to solve, what result you want, or simply what's not working right now — and we will ask the right questions to figure out the best solution together. A discovery call or WhatsApp chat is always a free first step.
What happens after I submit the contact form?▾
We review your message personally and respond within 24 hours via your preferred channel (email or WhatsApp). For service enquiries, we will either send a detailed proposal or ask a few clarifying questions first. There is no obligation — we are happy to discuss your needs before you commit to anything.
How do I visit the MPK Digital cyber café?▾
Walk-ins are welcome Monday through Saturday 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM, and Sunday 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM. For digital training sessions or assisted online applications, we recommend calling or WhatsApp-ing ahead to confirm staff availability and book your preferred time slot. Printing and internet access are always available without an appointment.
Is the initial consultation free?▾
Yes. All initial conversations — whether via email, WhatsApp, or a short discovery call — are completely free and carry no obligation. We will review your requirements, ask clarifying questions, and provide a written proposal before any work begins. You only commit when you are fully satisfied with the scope, timeline, and pricing.
