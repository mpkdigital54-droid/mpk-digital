Learning Hub Questions

Is all learning content on MPK Digital genuinely free?▾
Yes — every tutorial, guide, how-to article, and resource in the MPK Digital Learning Hub is 100% free with no registration required. We believe digital knowledge should be accessible to anyone in East Africa regardless of budget. Our paid offerings are professional digital services (Google Ads management, web development, AI automation) — not the educational content.
What level of digital knowledge do I need to start learning here?▾
None. The Beginner Resources section is designed for people who are completely new to digital — no assumed knowledge, no jargon without explanation. From there, Tutorials and How-To articles build progressively, and Guides go deepest for those who want comprehensive understanding. There is a clear path for every starting level.
Do you offer in-person digital training in Nairobi?▾
Yes. In addition to our free online learning content, MPK Digital offers in-person digital skills training at our Nairobi cyber café. Sessions cover computer basics, Microsoft Office, internet and email, Google Workspace, social media for business, and WhatsApp Business. Training is available one-on-one or in small groups — contact us to book.
