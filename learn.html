FAQ

Learning Hub Questions

Is all the learning content genuinely free?▾
Yes — every tutorial, guide, how-to article, and resource in the MPK Digital Learning Hub is 100% free with no registration required. We believe digital knowledge should be accessible to anyone in East Africa regardless of budget. Our paid offerings are professional digital services, not the educational content.
Do I need any prior knowledge to start learning here?▾
None at all. The Beginner Resources section is designed for people who are completely new to digital — no assumed knowledge. From there, Tutorials and How-To articles build progressively, and Guides go deepest for comprehensive understanding.
Do you offer in-person digital training in Nairobi?▾
Yes. In addition to free online content, MPK Digital offers in-person digital skills training at our Nairobi cyber café. Sessions cover computer basics, Microsoft Office, internet and email, Google Workspace, social media for business, and WhatsApp Business. Available one-on-one or in small groups — contact us to book.
