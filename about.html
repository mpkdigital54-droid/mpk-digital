About MPK Digital

Who We Are

MPK Digital is a Nairobi-based digital platform built on the belief that every person and business in East Africa deserves access to quality digital knowledge and professional digital support.

Our Story

Built for East Africa's Digital Economy

MPK Digital was founded with one clear purpose: to bridge the gap between digital knowledge and practical application for individuals, entrepreneurs, and businesses across East Africa.

We operate across three interconnected pillars — a digital media publication covering technology, AI, marketing, and business; a free learning hub with tutorials, guides, and how-to resources; and a professional digital services team delivering Google Ads management, web development, AI automation, and technical writing.

Our cyber café in Nairobi anchors us in the community — providing walk-in printing, internet access, online applications help, and personalised digital training for individuals who need direct, in-person digital support.

What distinguishes us is simple: we are practitioners. Every service we offer, we use ourselves. Every article we publish draws from real experience in East African digital contexts.
