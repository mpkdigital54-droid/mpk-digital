About MPK Digital

Who We Are

MPK Digital is a Nairobi-based digital platform built on the belief that every person and business in East Africa deserves access to quality digital knowledge and professional digital support.

Our Story

Built for East Africa's Digital Economy

MPK Digital was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between digital knowledge and practical application for individuals, entrepreneurs, and businesses across East Africa.

We operate across three interconnected pillars — a digital media publication covering technology, AI, marketing, and business; a free learning hub with tutorials, guides, and how-to resources; and a professional digital services team delivering Google Ads management, web development, AI automation, and technical writing to clients across Kenya.

Our cyber café in Nairobi anchors us in the community — providing walk-in printing, internet access, online applications help, and personalised digital training for individuals who need direct, in-person digital support.

What distinguishes us is simple: we are practitioners. Every service we offer, we use ourselves. Every article we publish draws from real experience in East African digital contexts. We don't copy-paste strategies from Silicon Valley and hope they work in Nairobi — we test, iterate, and document what actually works here.

FAQ

About MPK Digital

Is MPK Digital a media company or a digital agency?▾
MPK Digital is both — and more. We are a digital platform operating across three pillars: digital media (publishing technology, AI, and marketing content), a learning hub (free educational resources), and a digital services agency (Google Ads, web development, AI automation, technical writing). Our cyber café in Nairobi also provides in-person digital access services. This three-pillar model is intentional — each pillar strengthens the others.
Where is MPK Digital based?▾
MPK Digital is headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. Our cyber café and in-person services are available at our Nairobi location. Digital services — Google Ads, web development, AI automation, and technical writing — are delivered remotely to clients across Kenya, East Africa, and internationally.
Can I collaborate with or write for MPK Digital?▾
Yes. We welcome collaboration with digital professionals, entrepreneurs, educators, and tech experts across East Africa. This includes guest blog contributions, service partnerships, and content collaborations. Reach out via our Contact page with your proposal and we will respond promptly.
