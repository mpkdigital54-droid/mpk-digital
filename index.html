Frequently Asked Questions

What People Ask About MPK Digital

What is MPK Digital and what does it do?▾
MPK Digital is a Nairobi-based digital platform that operates across three pillars: digital media (technology, AI, marketing, and business articles), a learning hub (free tutorials, guides, and how-to resources), and professional digital services (Google Ads management, web development, AI automation, technical writing, and cyber café services). We serve individuals, entrepreneurs, and businesses across East Africa.
What digital services does MPK Digital offer in Kenya?▾
MPK Digital offers Google Ads campaign management, professional web development (including M-Pesa integration), AI workflow automation, technical writing and documentation, and cyber café services including printing, scanning, fast internet access, online applications help, and digital skills training. All services are available to clients across Kenya and East Africa, with cyber café services available in-person in Nairobi.
Is MPK Digital's learning content free?▾
Yes. All articles, tutorials, guides, and how-to resources in the MPK Digital Learning Hub are completely free. We believe digital knowledge should be accessible to everyone regardless of budget. Our paid offerings are the professional digital services (Google Ads, web development, AI automation, etc.) which are separate from the free content.
Where is MPK Digital located?▾
MPK Digital is based in Nairobi, Kenya. Our cyber café and in-person services are available at our Nairobi location. Digital services (Google Ads, web development, AI automation, and technical writing) are delivered remotely and are available to clients across Kenya, East Africa, and internationally.
How do I contact MPK Digital for a service enquiry?▾
You can reach MPK Digital by email at hello@mpkdigital.co.ke, via WhatsApp at +254 700 000 000, or by filling the contact form on our Contact page. We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent needs, WhatsApp is the fastest channel.
