FAQ

What People Ask About MPK Digital

What is MPK Digital and what does it do?▾
MPK Digital is a Nairobi-based digital platform combining tech media, a free learning hub, and professional digital services. We publish technology and business articles, offer free tutorials and guides, and provide Google Ads management, web development, AI automation, technical writing, and cyber café services to businesses across Kenya and East Africa.
What digital services does MPK Digital offer in Kenya?▾
MPK Digital offers Google Ads campaign management, professional web development (including M-Pesa integration), AI workflow automation, technical writing and documentation, plus cyber café services including printing, scanning, fast internet access, online applications help, and digital skills training in Nairobi.
Is the MPK Digital learning content free?▾
Yes — all tutorials, guides, how-to articles, and resources in the MPK Digital Learning Hub are completely free with no registration required. We believe digital knowledge should be accessible to everyone in East Africa regardless of budget.
Where is MPK Digital located?▾
MPK Digital is based in Nairobi, Kenya. Our cyber café and in-person services are available at our Nairobi location. Digital services (Google Ads, web development, AI automation, technical writing) are delivered remotely to clients anywhere in Kenya, East Africa, and internationally.
How do I contact MPK Digital?▾
Reach us by email at hello@mpkdigital.co.ke, via WhatsApp at +254 700 000 000, or through the contact form on our Contact page. We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on business days. WhatsApp is the fastest channel for urgent matters.
