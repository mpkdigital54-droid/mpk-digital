FAQ

Digital Services Questions

How much do MPK Digital's services cost?▾
Pricing depends on your specific project scope. We provide clear, written quotes before any work begins — no hidden fees. Contact us with your project details and we'll respond with a detailed proposal within 24 hours. We work with businesses across a wide range of budgets.
Do you work with clients outside Nairobi?▾
Absolutely. Google Ads management, web development, AI automation, and technical writing are all delivered remotely and available to clients anywhere in Kenya, East Africa, and internationally. Only our cyber café walk-in services require physical presence in Nairobi.
How long does a website take to build?▾
A standard business website or landing page typically takes 5–10 business days. E-commerce stores or more complex builds take 2–4 weeks. We provide a specific timeline in your project proposal. Timeline depends on how quickly you can provide content, feedback, and approvals.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and international payments via PayPal or Wise for clients outside Kenya. Payment terms are agreed in writing before work begins. For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit to start.
