FAQ

Digital Services Questions

How much do MPK Digital's services cost?▾
Pricing depends on your specific project scope and requirements. We provide clear, written quotes before any work begins — no hidden fees or surprise charges. Contact us with your project details and we will respond with a detailed proposal within 24 hours. We work with businesses across a range of budgets.
Do you work with clients outside Nairobi?▾
Absolutely. Google Ads management, web development, AI automation, and technical writing services are all delivered remotely and are available to clients anywhere in Kenya, East Africa, and internationally. Only our cyber café walk-in services require physical presence in Nairobi. We communicate via WhatsApp, email, Zoom, or Google Meet.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept M-Pesa (Till or Send Money), bank transfer, and international payments via PayPal or Wise for clients outside Kenya. Payment terms are agreed in writing before work begins. For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit to commence work.
How long does a website take to build?▾
A standard business website or landing page typically takes 5–10 business days from brief to launch. E-commerce stores or more complex builds take 2–4 weeks. We will give you a specific timeline in your project proposal. Timeline depends on how quickly you can provide content, feedback, and approvals.
Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?▾
Yes. We offer ongoing support and maintenance retainers for websites, active Google Ads campaigns, and automated systems. For Google Ads specifically, ongoing management is standard since campaigns require continuous optimisation to perform. We outline all support options clearly in every project proposal.
