FAQ

Questions About Our Blog

How often does MPK Digital publish new blog posts?▾
We publish 2–3 new articles per week across our blog and media hub. Topics rotate between technology news, AI tools, digital marketing strategies, and business insights — all with a specific East African context and angle.
Can I contribute a guest post to MPK Digital?▾
Yes! We welcome guest contributions from digital professionals, entrepreneurs, and tech experts based in East Africa. Articles must be original, at least 800 words, and provide genuinely useful insights for our audience. Submit your pitch via our Contact page.
What is the difference between the Blog and the Media Hub?▾
The MPK Digital Blog focuses on opinion pieces, case studies, how-to guides, and behind-the-scenes content from our own work and experiences. The Media Hub covers broader industry news, technology trends, and in-depth category coverage (Technology, AI, Digital Marketing, Digital Business, Media & Culture). Both are free and interconnected.
Are MPK Digital's blog articles accurate and well-researched?▾
Yes. Every article on MPK Digital is written or reviewed by digital practitioners with hands-on experience in the East African market. We cite sources, use real data where available, and clearly distinguish between our opinions and established facts. We update articles when information becomes outdated.
